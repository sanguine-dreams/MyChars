I have always been one to journal and document my days. I supposed I used these pages to vent words I would not otherwise say out loud. Later, it became a way of keeping track of all the insane things I was doing. I didn’t want to miss a thing when I retold the wild tales…
Today I would like to summarize. The things I write, you likely already know. If you did not, well… it soon won’t matter that you do.
My name is Datura. I am a Shadowscale, although a Lukiel- some will very quickly point out. I was born with a deformity on my right leg that made physical strain a nightmare and training at Archon torture. But I wasn’t alone, the man who would become my father, a Dark Brotherhood member, was studying alchemy there to improve his craft. He saw a daughter in me and I’m eternally grateful. He trained me in poison, which I crafted medicine out of, fit only for Argonians like myself.
This allowed me to prove myself enough that Archon let me follow the Dark Brotherhood Assassin to his sanctuary. We lived in Bravil, and I finally found some relative peace. I worked on my craft, developed new skills. Till news of the Purification of the Cheydinhal Sanctuary arrived.
I moved there, an Assassin rank taking up the mantle of an Executioner- with none of the respect. It was tiring, thankless work. That is why once they no longer needed me, I decided to take a trip and explore Tamriel. I had never really been outside the dank caverns of Bravil and the swamps of Archon much. It was a good opportunity.
I immediately came across adventures, out on a prophecy to Azura herself. I should have walked the other bloody way.
Alas, we slaughtered minotaurs, I stole away a baby minotaur to the Brotherhood’s ranks- they still think I sold her. Then we time travelled! It sounds as odd writing it as it was experiencing it. Several dead Daedric champions, a failed relationship, and a chopped off leg later- it was over. I got a beautiful ring out of it, it suits me well. I believe it may not have been the leg that made me grotesque, but my hist-less soul.

-Dearly yours, Executioner Datura
